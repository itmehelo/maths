Who am I?

I'm Andy Tran, a Math major and aspiring teacher at CSULB. I'm aiming for a Master's of Science in Math - wish me luck!

What's this website for?

Two reasons: I think it'd be cool to have physical documentation of my progress as I work toward my degree. I wanted to start a website and possibly help out classmates or other people who are learning math.

What will you post on here?

My goal for the website at the start is to work through problems and explain them the best I can. I'll go over concepts and review them as I learn them. I hope to continue to learn so I can talk more about cooler math concepts too!
